              Armor Set 25.4 Weight

              All-Knowing Set is an Armor Set in Elden Ring. All-Knowing Set is a Medium Weight Armor that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.

               

              Where to find All-Knowing Set in Elden Ring

              Obtained upon defeating Sir Gideon Ofnir, the All-Knowing in Leyndell, Ashen Capital. [Map Link] Video Location

               

              All-Knowing Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring

              • All-Knowing Helm
              • All-Knowing Armor
              • All-Knowing Gauntlets
              • All-Knowing Greaves

               

              All-Knowing Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips

              • In addition to the sculpted ornamentation, the articulated joints on the plackart, poleyns, couters, and gauntlets are designed to look like eyes that open and close as the wearer moves.
              • The All-Knowing Armor can be altered to the All-Knowing Armor (Altered) to remove the cloak.
              • Poise updated to patch 1.07. See Patch Notes for details.
