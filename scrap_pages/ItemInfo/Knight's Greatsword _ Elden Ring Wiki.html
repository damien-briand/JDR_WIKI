              Knight's Greatsword is a Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Knight's Greatsword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for melee combat. Its skill, Stamp (Upward Cut) also allows the user to prevent recoil from enemy attacks and follow up with another strong attack.

               

              Greatsword bestowed upon skilled swordsmen in the demigods' service, and yet had no rank or title.

              Featuring a long grip enabling a dual-handed use, this weapon was designed to dispatch larger foes. Being charged with such a duty was undoubtedly a great honor.

               

              Where to Find Knight's Greatsword in Elden Ring

              The Knight's Greatsword weapon can be found at the following location:

              • Dropped by most enemies in the knight archetype such as:
                • Godrick Knights (throughout Limgrave)
                • Cuckoo Knights (throughout Liurnia of the Lakes)
                • Mausoleum Knight
                • Leyndell Knights (throughout the Altus Plateau, especially in Leyndell, Royal Capital)
                • Redmane Knights (throughout Caelid, especially around Redmane Castle)
              • An easy-to-farm Mausoleum Knight that drops this weapon can be found just outside the Black Knife Catacombs in Liurnia of the Lakes.

               

              Elden Ring Knight's Greatsword Notes & Tips

              • Weapon Skill: Stamp (Upward Cut)
              • This weapon can be infused with Ashes of War
              • Can be buffed by Magic or Consumables
              • Knight's Greatsword can be upgraded by using Smithing Stones.
              • The Knight's Greatsword is the 2nd longest weapon in its class. 
              • Sell Value:   200
              • Base drop rate is 4% (at 100 Discovery)
              • NOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed. Please refer to the Upgrades Table of this weapon below. Additionally, speed, range, recovery time and guard counter speed of Greatswords weapons has increased.
